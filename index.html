Our company was founded in 1984 and is led by an experienced team of entrepreneurs and professionals of the financial sector.

Their vision was to create a company that offers its clients wide-ranging innovative asset management solutions. Today, we continue to apply this vision to help our clients achieve consistent long term financial performance.

Our objective approach underpinned by our high standards of personal service, our commitment to build and preserve the wealth of our clients, and our belief in the importance of a robust investment process has remained unchanged. This ability to continually adapt and evolve has held us at the forefront of the industry for over four decades.

We are now one of Switzerland’s leading wealth management firms. We offer a wide selection of wealth planning and asset management services to charities, trusts and professional institutes, but our core business remains the same as it was over 37 years ago, the delivery of a high-quality, personal service to our private clients.

We now commands around £3.5 billion of assets under management. We continue to increase our growth rate by selecting the best in class investments and other lucrative product options for our expanding global client base.
